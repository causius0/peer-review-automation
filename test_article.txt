This is a test article about personalized medicine, genetic testing, and health data sharing in the European Union. The study surveyed 6,581 respondents from eight EU countries investigating public knowledge of personalized medicine, support for implementing genetic testing, and willingness to share health data. Results showed that 12.11% had high compound knowledge, 81.5% supported genetic testing, and 52.35% were willing to share health data for altruistic use.
